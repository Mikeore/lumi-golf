# Current Evidence from LUMI

This repository is motivated by results from my broader LUMI research project.

The strongest current small-scale signals are:

- reliable grokking-style success on modular arithmetic across multiple seeds
- a structural holdout benchmark where the architecture consistently outperforms a baseline on balanced accuracy
- evidence that compact, structure-biased modeling can produce useful generalization gains under constrained settings

These are not direct Parameter Golf results. They are the motivation for creating a dedicated compact language-model branch tailored to the challenge.
